Your Journey with Redmond Spine Center

You don't feel the way you should.We want to fix that.

No longer should you live with pain. Working with us means we focus directly on finding the source of your pain and addressing that issue to help you recover, and keep you healthy. Here's how I approach each and every case:

Step 1

We always start with a conversation.

Great chiropractic care starts with a conversation. This may sound odd, but we aren’t concerned with your pain. We are concerned about what is causing the pain, and what the pain is keeping you from doing.

Step 2

We focus on root causes of your pain.

Chiropractic care is not just to make you feel better, but to heal better. Yes, we want you to feel less pain, but more importantly, we want to make you heal and get back to doing what you love.

Step 3

Let’s leverage the right tools for the right treatment.

We want to give ourselves the best chance of success, so we invest in our tools: Full onsite digital Xray with digitized X-ray analysis, rehab equipment, soft-tissue tools, and any resource you need to heal naturally.

Step 4

Now we build a care plan designed specifically for you.

Let’s put it all together. We’ll take the information from our conversation about your health history and goals, connect them with our evaluation findings, and create a customized plan that’s tailored to your needs.
